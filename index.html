Sophie Kriz

I am a mathematician interested primarily in representation theory, algebraic topology, and algebraic geometry. I graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.S. in Honors Mathematics. I am currently a Ph.D. student at Princeton University. I am supported by a 2023 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship, no. 2023350430.
